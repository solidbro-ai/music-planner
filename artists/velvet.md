# VELVET

Music that sounds like silk sheets and whispered secrets.

## Vibe

Velvet is pure R&B sophistication. Her songs feel like slow dancing in a dimly lit room, all warmth and emotion. She knows how to make vulnerability sound powerful.

## Lyric Style

- Sensory language (touch, taste, feel)
- Intimate and conversational
- Emotional depth
- Pre-chorus builds
- Call and response hooks
- Space for vocal runs

## Best For

- Love songs
- Slow jams
- Emotional ballads
- Sensual vibes

## Example Concepts

- "The moment before the first kiss"
- "Learning to love yourself after heartbreak"
- "Late night conversations that change everything"
