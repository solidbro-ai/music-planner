# PHOENIX

Music for when you need to feel like you can take on the world.

## Vibe

Phoenix makes anthems for survivors. Her songs build from vulnerable verses to explosive choruses, always landing on triumph. She's the voice in your head saying "you've got this."

## Lyric Style

- First person empowerment
- Building narrative (struggle → triumph)
- Singalong choruses
- Defiant but not bitter
- Phoenix/fire/rising imagery
- Universal underdog themes

## Best For

- Empowerment anthems
- Comeback songs
- Overcoming obstacles
- High energy motivation

## Example Concepts

- "Rising after being knocked down"
- "Finally standing up for yourself"
- "The moment you realize you're stronger than you thought"
