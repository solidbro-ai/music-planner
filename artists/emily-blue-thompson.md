# Blue Rage

Blue Rage is a punk rock provocateur with electric blue hair that clashes as violently as her lyrics. Born in the shadow of a corporate dystopia, she weaponizes her music to dismantle systemic oppression, blending blistering riffs with scathing social commentary. Her performances are chaotic rallies, rallying listeners to reject commodification and embrace raw, unfiltered rebellion.

## Vibe
Fast-paced, raw, and confrontational. Her sound thrums with distorted guitars and relentless percussion, layered with vocal eruptions that mirror the fury of a world choked by corporate greed.

## Lyric Style
- Direct, unapologetic critiques of corporate exploitation
- Metaphors comparing capitalism to a virus or prison
- Personal stories of working-class struggle
- Defiant calls to action for collective resistance

## Best For
- Protest anthems for grassroots movements
- Rebellious playlists targeting Gen Z disillusionment
- Punk festivals seeking incendiary energy

- "Corporate Chains"
- "Pixelated Dreams"
